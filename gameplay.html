Gameplay

Dr. Mario is a puzzle game in a similar style to Tetris. The game is played on a 8x16 board, with 2 exceptions: Dr. Mario (Game Boy) on a 8x15, and Dr. Mario World on a 8x11

Pills will appear from the top-centre of the board and fall down, pressing left/right move the pills Left/Right, Down moves the pills down faster, A rotates the pill clockwise, B rotates the pill counter-clockwise, and in some of the newer games Up makes the pill go as far down as they can go instantly.

Every pill has 2 segments, and will choose 1 of the colour sets: Red, Blue, and Yellow; or Magenta, Cyan, and Green, and viruses will have colours corresponding to the pill's, when 4+ of the same colour line up the pills and viruses will disappear and award points. If the other half of the pills (assuming they were not used to achieve the 4+ in a row) will fall, if the fallen pills clear more viruses you will create a combo, which will award bonus points (and send garbage in multiplayer). The points are as follows.

Combo Points Garbage
0 200 0
1 400 2
2 800 3
3 1600 4
4 3200 5

The number of viruses correspond to the current level. 0 - 4, 5 - 24, 10 - 44, etc. The highest level is 24 on most releases.

Gameplay (Dr. Mario World)

Dr. Mario World is a puzzle game similar to Tetris. the game is played on a 8x11 board

Pills are located at the bottom of the screen in a circle you can drag the pills to where you want, but after they move up a space they can't go back down, and tapping the pill will rotate it clockwise.

Every pill has 2 segments, for normal levels the colours are: Red, Blue, and Yellow; and for challenge levels: Magenta, Cyan, and Green, and viruses will have colours corresponding to the pill's, when 3+ of the same colour line up the pills and viruses will disappear and award points. If the other half of the pills (assuming they were not used to achieve the 4+ in a row) will fall, and the sub pills can be move the same way as whole pills (minus rotating).

In multiplayer after clearing enough viruses, or clearing all the viruses, will send you opponent more lines of viruses, if all the viruses are cleared you will receive 2 extra lines of viruses. The first one to hit the bottom of the screen loses.
